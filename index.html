Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has the potential for innovation, but its performance is lower than many other countries. However, there have been many innovations and initiatives in recent years.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is vulnerable to climate change and environmental degradation. The country has made progress in developing sustainable policies, but it still faces challenges in achieving sustainability.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh ranks highest in Infrastructure (86th), Creative outputs (88th) and Market sophistication, Knowledge and technology outputs (92nd).

Empowerment

Bangladesh has made significant progress in empowering women, particularly in education, labor force participation, and political representation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
